Love to listen to music?

MUSIC (from the Greek musike, literally - the art of muses), a type of art in which musical sounds organized in a certain way serve as a means of embodying artistic images. The main elements and expressive means of music are mode, rhythm, meter, tempo, loud dynamics, timbre, melody, harmony, polyphony, instrumentation. Music is recorded in musical notation and realized in the process of performance. The division of music into secular and spiritual is accepted. The main area of sacred music is cult music (the oldest extant music is Buddhist ritual music). The development of the European musical theory of musical notation and musical pedagogy is connected with European cult music (usually called church music). By performing means, music is divided into vocal (singing), instrumental and vocal-instrumental. Music is often combined with choreography, theatrical art, cinema.

How is music created?

In fact, music can be written with only a computer, i.e. it is not necessary to have a guitar, synthesizer, etc., you can just download the program and make music, or something similar to it. These programs are called "digital audio workstation", but we will call them DAWs. Our DAWs are designed to record, store, process, and play back a signal, in our case, music. But not everything is so easy, you can't just go into the program, press 3 buttons, and that's it, the music will be ready, you need to figure it out a little here. it is also the easiest kind of musical activity - another confirmation of this are countless examples of musically illiterate people who created some of the most famous melodies. But the exception only proves the rule. Therefore, if hit melodies do not haunt you every day, then you will have to “catch” them yourself. And for this you need to work hard. In general terms, creating music is the same process as creating a building, painting, book, etc.

Why do people listen to music?

Many people listen to music while studying, working. It makes us better, inspires and inspires us, gives unforgettable feelings. It is thanks to music that a person is able to relax, rest, get certain food for the mind. This type of art is able to radically change the mood of a person - to cheer him up, sadden him, feel longing. Music is a source of a wide variety of emotions and feelings.

The music provided to you on the screen is usually turned on during work and study, because it does not distract, does not take all the attention.
